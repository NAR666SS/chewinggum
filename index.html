Q&A

YOU ASKED.

Does this actually grow your jaw?
Mastic gum increases load on the masseter — the muscle that gives the jaw its definition. Like any muscle, it hypertrophies under resistance. Don't expect surgery results, expect muscle results: visible in 4–8 weeks of daily chewing.
How is this different from supermarket gum?
Supermarket gum is sugar + softeners. It melts in 90 seconds. Mastic gum is tree resin — naturally 4× firmer, lasts 5–8 hours, contains zero artificial sweeteners. The chew itself is the entire point.
Will it really not smell?
Correct. Mastic has a faint pine note for the first 10 minutes, then becomes flavorless and odorless. You can chew it during a presentation. Nobody will know.
How many pieces a day?
Most users chew 2–3 pieces per day, 30–60 minutes per session, alternating sides. One tin (28 pieces) lasts about 2 weeks at that pace.
